Why Join NSBE FAU

Professional Network

Connect with peers, alumni, and employers who support your academic and career goals.

Scholarships

Gain access to national scholarships and chapter-supported funding opportunities.

Convention Access

Participate in the NSBE Annual Convention to meet top employers and attend career workshops.

Community

Join a welcoming chapter built on mentorship, shared success, and student leadership.

•

Become a Member

To join NSBE FAU, attend a general body meeting, register on the NSBE national portal, and pay chapter dues. Our chapter welcomes all FAU students who want to grow through engineering and community involvement.

General Body Meetings

General Body Meetings are held biweekly and are open to all FAU students. These meetings are the best way to stay connected, learn about upcoming events, and meet our community.
