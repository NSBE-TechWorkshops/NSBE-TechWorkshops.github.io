Programs & Events

Discover how NSBE FAU builds technical skills, connects members to industry, and supports leadership growth.

NSBE Annual Convention

Largest gathering of Black engineers

The National NSBE Convention is the world’s largest event for Black engineers, with a career fair, workshops, competitions, scholarships, and networking with Fortune 500 companies. NSBE FAU supports members as they attend, connect with employers, and represent our chapter.

•

Technical Workshops

Hands-on coding practice

Workshops cover coding, engineering tools, resume building, and technical interview preparation.

•

Social Gatherings

Networking & culture

Enjoy mixers, game nights, cultural celebrations, and team-building events that strengthen our chapter community.

•

Community Outreach

K-12 STEM support

We host outreach programs and mentorship initiatives to inspire younger students to pursue STEM pathways.

•

Professional Development

Career-focused support

Members attend company info sessions, alumni panels, and career fairs to build professional confidence and connections.
