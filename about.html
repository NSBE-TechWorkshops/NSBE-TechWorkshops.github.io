About Us

NSBE FAU supports Black engineers at every stage with academic resources, leadership training, and community-driven programs.

•

Who We Are

NSBE FAU is dedicated to increasing the number of culturally responsible Black engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally, and positively impact the community. We deliver mentoring, networking, and leadership opportunities for all students at FAU.

•

Our History

Founded to support Black engineering students at Florida Atlantic University, our chapter has grown steadily through partnership with FAU's College of Engineering and Computer Science. Today we host workshops, outreach events, and professional development activities for students across majors.

•

Our Goals

Academic Excellence

Support members through study groups, tutoring, and resources to maintain strong GPAs.

Professional Development

Prepare students for careers with resume workshops, interview practice, and company connections.

Community Outreach

Engage with K-12 students and the local community through STEM events and volunteer work.

Brotherhood & Sisterhood

Create a supportive network through social events, mentorship, and leadership opportunities.

•

National NSBE Connection

As a campus chapter of the National Society of Black Engineers, NSBE FAU connects members with national resources, scholarships, conventions, and regional leadership. Members gain access to a strong support network and career-building opportunities across the broader NSBE community.

•

FAU Partnership

We work closely with FAU's College of Engineering and Computer Science to provide students with academic support, career pathways, and collaborative events that amplify the student experience.
